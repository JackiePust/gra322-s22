Six Under Animation

Six Under is a fishing and tackle company idea that was inspired by the combination of a letter and a number to build a brand. For this project, I selected the number six and the letter "u," and experimented with different ways I could showcase these letterforms together. After developing a solid direction, I worked on applying the idea of a fish hook to the logo to capture the meaning behind the brand and convey the brand's uses to customers.
