Candier Candle Website

For this project, I redesigned and created a new website platform for the candle company, Candier. I not only worked on highlighting the values of the brand, but I wanted to further exemplify them through the use of color and a fluid grid system. In addition to the desktop version, I also tested different ways to show this website through a mobile platform. In including photography and bright colors, I increased legibility and visual display of the product.
