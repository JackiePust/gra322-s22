Martin Luther King, Junior Campaign

This project was completed as a team of four, where I worked alongside fellow designers to create and assemble a campaign for Arizona State University's annual Martin Luther King, Junior campaign. When developing this campaign, our team emphasized King's values of equality, freedom, justice, and love, representing each idea as a key to unlock something bigger than one's self. Within this campaign, our team created a new website design, email invites for participants, and a television campaign to be displayed accross all ASU campuses. For this campaign, metal keys would be engraved with the four values and given to students and faculty at the Martin Luther King, Junior march on west campus, as well as the annual Martin Luther King, Junior breakfast and award ceremony. This allows individuals to not only remember the importance of this event and movement, but also reminds key holders to hold tight to these values and practice them through their daily lives as a way to open up new chapters and doors to a brighter future.
