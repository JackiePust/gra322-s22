Our Story

Charlie’s Café, one of Sun Devil Dining’s local restaurant partners, brings the taste of Europe to ASU! Proudly serving Firecreek Coffee and featuring fresh European sand- wiches and baked goods. Come experience the difference! With three locations on campus, there is always a Charlie’s Cafe near! Charlie’s Café is located at the Hayden Library, Bio Design B Building and the College of Design.
